Christian the Dog

My dogs name is Christian. He is a pomeranian breed and light brown in color.

About Him

  • He is buoyant in deportment, inquisitive by nature, cocky, commanding, and animated
  • He is vivacious and spirited, bold and brash
  • He is a sharp-eyed busy body who checks out every sight, sound, and activity tells me what he thinks about it
  • He is delightfully alive and aware of everything going on around him
  • He is keenly alert to approaching strangers
  • He is fine with other pets but attempts to chase strange dogs regardless of size
  • He is not inclined to take orders from anyone whom he views as below himself in importance
  • He loves to take walks and run around and he'll ensure to mark his territory every step
  • He is not so fond of water so taking a bath becomes an issue
  • He loves being around people and gets easily bored when left alone
  • He hates loud noises that come from fireworks, thunderstorms and helicopter as they scare him

    • Grooming

    I wash my dog at home because i prefer to and it gives us enough time to bond which i love. He doesn't fancy a bath so i'll be chasing him when he discovers its bath time but once we get started he does not mind it at all.

    Here are some of my grooming habits

    • I trim his nails when i notice they've become to long
    • I use shampoo and conditioner that isn't harsh to wash him and sometimes a tick and flea medicine
    • I clean his ears regularly though he doesnt like it
    • I brush his teeth to prevent dental issues
    • I brush his fur and trim it once in a while because he sheds alot

    As much as i love to groom him at home, I take him to the vet every 3-6months to ensure his health is okay and he is free of ticks
